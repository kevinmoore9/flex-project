This paper-trading app is easy to use. Find it on the iOS app store, and download it for free.

Sign up with email and a password

Once signed in, you will be able to search for stocks that are available on the NASDAQ exchange. Click on the stock symbol to see detailed information, and buy or sell.

In order to buy stocks, you must first deposit money into your account. Don't worry, this is only paper trading so making a deposit is easy, and does not cost any money.

Navigate to your dashboard in order to see a breakdown of your portfolio. This show a chart of your account's value over time, what stocks you own,and how much money is in your count.

Gain market trading experience
in your spare moments

This app was built for iOS with React Native. The stock data made available to users comes from the Yahoo Finance API

This app also communicates with a backend built with Ruby on Rails that features a Postgres database and makes use of the Jbuilder Gem to send JSON objects to the front end to update account information. The backend stores account information and makes timed calls to the Yahoo finance API to update user account equity balances and stock values at the end of each trading day.
